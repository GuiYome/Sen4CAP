<SEN4CAP system is designed to automatically generate four 
key products for agriculture monitoring, based on Sentinel-1 and Sentinel-2 
and which are relevant for the Common Agricultural Policy: biophysical indicators
describing the vegetative development of crops, cultivated crop type maps, 
grassland mowing detection product, agricultural practices monitoring product. 
It was developed by the Sen4CAP project, which has been funded by the European 
Space Agency (EOEP-5 programme)>
